About

BusCrowdTrack is an intelligent monitoring platform that brings transparency to rural public transport. It combines computer vision, live GPS data, and route intelligence to give operators and passengers a clear, real-time picture of every journey.

Live Location

Displays the real‑time position of each bus using data from the live_location collection (longitude & latitude).

Passenger Count

Tracks how many passengers are currently on board using the passenger_events collection (inside_total field).

Route & Stops

Visualizes every stop along the route from the bus_stops collection, helping planners and passengers understand the full journey.
